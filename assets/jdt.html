Established in 1994, JDT Islam Polytechnic College has earned its reputation as one of the finest polytechnic institutes in the state. Approved by AICTE and the Government of Kerala, this institute offers exemplary technical education at the diploma level, making a decade-long journey of surpassing excellence in the educational landscape.

Message to us.
Owing to rapid social and technological changes taking place around us, there is increasing need for quality engineers who can think creatively, apply their intelligence to solve problems and to create new designs and products for the ever changing world. Our mission is to mould highly competent technical professionals with wide range of analytic, strategic and leadership skills capable of facing the challenges of the technical world. With the above objective in mind, we are taking various steps to build. First we build a strong foundation for the first year students and subsequently provide various trainings to enable them to catch up the Engineering curriculum. We make use of Information and Communication Technology to a great extent for the development of soft skill, life skill and technical skills and make our students globally competitive.
